The Best Movies of All Time According to Akash

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Avenger's Endgame

Best Animation and Storyline of All time.Really cool sci-fi movie by Marvel Studios.

Uri: The Surgical Strike

Depicting Major Vihaan Singh Shergill of the Indian Army leads a covert operation against a group of militants who attacked a base in Uri, Kashmir, in 2016 and killed many soldiers.

The Dark Knight Rises

Really cool animation.Best acting and storyline.
